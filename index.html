Our Story Services

Started Since 1977

Live music in the greens

My musical journey began in 1977 in Kanpur, where I first picked up the Hawaiian guitar while balancing my studies. Life's path led me to Delhi, where I began performing professionally under the name Eagle Band.

As time went on, my passion for music only grew deeper. I expanded my skills, learning to play the keyboard, harmonium, and several other instruments. Over the past 35 years, I've had the privilege of performing across India and overseas, sharing my music with diverse audiences.

For the last 7 years, my focus has been on Ghazal evenings and retro Bollywood performances, reviving the timeless charm of classic melodies. In the past 3 years, I've also embraced spiritual gatherings, bringing soulful music to devotional settings.

Music has been more than a profession—it has been my lifelong companion and purpose.
